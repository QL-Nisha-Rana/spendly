Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is needed to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not integrate with any advertising or analytics networks that access your personal information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
